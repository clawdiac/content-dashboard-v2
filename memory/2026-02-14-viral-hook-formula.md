**Source:** Oliver Henry + Larry analysis  
**Pattern:** 50K-234K views when formula is applied

---

## The Formula

### Core Structure:
```
[Another Person] + [Conflict/Doubt/Barrier] → [Showed them/Proved wrong] → [Changed their mind/Now a believer]
```

### Why It Works:
- Creates a micro-story before the user swipes
- Taps into: curiosity, vindication, social proof, FOMO
- NOT about features, price, or app mechanics
- About the **human reaction**, the moment of realization

## Hook Brainstorm Questions (From Larry)

**Ask these for EVERY hook:**

1. **Who's the other person?** (not you)
   - Older relative? Friend? Skeptic? Authority figure?
   - The more relatable, the better

2. **What's their initial position?** (the doubt/barrier/conflict)
   - "They didn't believe..." / "They said no..." / "They thought it was impossible..."
   - Make it specific, not vague

3. **What's the moment of proof?** (what changes their mind)
   - Showing them the transformation
   - Revealing the result
   - Proving them wrong with evidence

4. **What's their reaction?** (the payoff)
   - Implied amazement / vindication / revelation
   - User imagines their face when they see it

## Anti-Patterns (What Fails: <10K Views)

### ❌ Self-Focused Hooks
- "See your room in 12+ styles before you commit" → 879 views
- "The difference between $500 and $5000 taste" → 2,671 views
- Feature talk, price comparisons, your accomplishment

### ❌ Unclear/Confusing Hooks
- "Why does my flat look like a student loan" → 905 views
- Too clever, doesn't create a clear picture

### ❌ No Relatable Conflict
- Generic transformation hooks without a human moment
- Just showcasing the feature, not the story

### ❌ Negative Openers (For Casino/Crypto)
- Avoid pure loss narratives (depressing)
- Better: Vindication over doubt than dwelling on failure

## Testing & Iteration

**Test 3-5 variations:**
1. Generate 3-5 different hooks from the same transformation
2. Post them on different days
3. Track views, engagement, conversions
4. Document what worked in your skill file
5. Update the pattern

**Example test set for NiggaBets:**
- Hook A: "My homie didn't believe in the odds until..."
- Hook B: "My girl said casinos were fake until she saw..."

Document the winner + winner margin.

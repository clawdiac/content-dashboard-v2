# Learnings

Document mistakes, lessons, and insights to avoid repeating errors.

## Format

When you learn something important:
1. Create a file: `lesson-YYYY-MM-DD.md`
2. Document: What happened, why it matters, what to do next time
3. Link it to relevant projects

## Examples

- **timing-matters.md** — What we learned about posting times
- **avoid-oversaturation.md** — Content frequency best practices
- **viral-formula.md** — What actually makes content go viral

Create your first entry when you have a lesson to capture!
